Beans on Toast

Description

You cant beat a classic like Beans on Toast. You cant get much simpler

Ingredients

  • Tin of baked beans
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Warm the grill up to a medium to high heat
  2. Pop a piece of bread in the toaster
  3. Heat a tins of beans in the microwave for 2 minute 30 seconds
  4. pour cooked beans over toast
