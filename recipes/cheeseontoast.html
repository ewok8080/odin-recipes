Cheese on Toast

Description

You cant beat a classic like cheese on Toast. You cant get much simpler

Ingredients

  • Strong Mature cheder
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Warm the grill up to a medium to high heat
  2. Slice enough cheese to cover cover bread
  3. Toast one side of the bread then remove from grill
  4. Place sliced cheese on the un-toasted side of the bread and place under the grill
  5. Keep an eye on the cheese and remove from grill once melted and bubbling
